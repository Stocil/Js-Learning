Horse

The domestic horse is an animal of the family of ungulates, domesticated and the only surviving subspecies of wild horse, extinct in the wild, with the exception of a small population of Przewalski's horse.

Donkey

The domestic donkey (Latin Equus asinus asinus), or donkey, is a domesticated subspecies of the wild donkey (Equus asinus), which played an important historical role in the development of human economy and culture and is still widely used in the economy of many developing countries.

Cat

A cat, or a domestic cat (Latin Felis silvestris catus), is a pet, one of the most popular (along with a dog) "companion animals". Being a solitary hunter of rodents and other small animals, a cat is a social animal that uses a wide range of sound signals for communication.
